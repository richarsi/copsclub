Welcome to COPS

by Jane Leigh — last modified Oct 28, 2013 07:26 PM — History
Welcome

We are currently Cambridgeshire’s premier competitive swimming club. Our programme is a partnership between Peterborough City Council, Vivacity and City of Peterborough Swim Squad. Our aim is to compete at the highest levels in the sport, and within our squads we have International swimmers and National medallists. We also compete at county and regional levels and travel widely to attend various open meets through-out the year. We are currently ranked in the top 10 clubs in England.

For any training related enquiries please email Head Coach, Ben Negus

For any general enquiries or correspondence please email our Honorary Secretary, Jane Leigh

or for written correspondence send to:- COPS Honorary Secretary, Regional Fitness and Swimming Centre, Bishops Road Peterborough PE1 5BW

Any enquiries about COPS Open Meets should be directed to Matt Newton

If you would like to comment on the website content or make suggestions for further development please do so by contacting us
